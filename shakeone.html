PB Oat Shake

Description:

This recipe provides an excessive amount of calories and protein for muscle growth and weight gain. Non-dairy milks such as almond may be used in place of whole milk, but will lower the total calories. Ingredients can be tailored to your specific taste after some experimentation. This shake will generally be around 1000 calories, with well over 60 grams of protein, depending on the amount of ingredients used.

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Banana
  • Blueberries
  • Yogurt
  • Protein
  • Peanut Butter
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Blend oats
  2. Add two scoops of protein powder
  3. Pour milk in blender
  4. Add peanut butter
  5. Add yogurt and fruit
  6. Blend fully
  7. Enjoy!
